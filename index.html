Climate-Smart Agriculture


DARJYO aims to raise the awareness and strengthen the capacities of key stakeholders in South Africa on the concepts, approaches, and tools for enabling climate-smart agriculture in the country.
##### Principles of Climate Smart Agriculture
related to climate change and climate-smart agriculture in South Africa's agricultural sector.
##### Climate-Smart Crop Production
explores how CSA differs from mainstream agriculture in crop production and why it is essential to adopt CSA given the impact of climate change.
##### Climate-Smart Livestock Production
focuses on the importance of livestock production for food security, the link between climate change and livestock production, ways to adapt livestock production to climate change.
##### Climate-Smart Remote Sensing
concepts and their application in the agriculture domain.

Benefits of Climate-Smart Practices

  • Improved Soil Health
  • Reduced Water Usage
  • Increased Crop Yields
  • Enhanced Climate Resilience
